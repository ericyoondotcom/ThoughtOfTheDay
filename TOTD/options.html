Thought Of The Day New Tab: Options

About

Thought of the Day New Tab will show a random thought on your new tab page. The thought will change every day.
The thought will change according to UTC time, so depending on your timezone, it may not change at midnight local time.

This chrome extension was made by Yoonicode.

This chrome extension is open source. View on GitHub.

Settings

Dark mode
Change thought every tab (instead of daily)
