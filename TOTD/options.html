Thought Of The Day New Tab: Options

About

Thought of the Day New Tab will show a random thought on your new tab page. The thought will change every day.
The thought may not change at night in your time zone, as it uses GMT.

This chrome extension was made by Eric Yooniverse at Yooniverse Games.

This chrome extension is open source, liscensed under the MIT liscence. View the repository on Github!
